About Audi-Hose

Audi-Hose is a free and open-source (FOSS) alternative to some name-brand audio feedback systems used by creators to provide a mechanism of voice communication. With Audi-Hose, it's easy for your audience to submit quick recorded messages to you for use in your podcast, radio-show, stream, vlog, or any other media.

Audi-Hose is licensed as AGPL-3.0, and is available freely on Github.
